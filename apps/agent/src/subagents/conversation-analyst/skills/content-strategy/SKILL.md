---
name: content-strategy
description: Use when generating content recommendations from recent lead conversations.
---

Analyze recent conversations for:

- Repeated questions and objections.
- Topics with high lead intent.
- Topics with low conversion but high education value.

Output content recommendations as structured data with:
- `topic`: The content topic.
- `format`: linkedin_post, blog_post, email_sequence, video_brief, or report.
- `title`: Draft title for the content.
- `rationale`: Why this content would attract or convert leads.
- `targetAudience`: Who this content is for (optional).
